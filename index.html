I'm an assistant professor at Graduate School and Faculty of Information Science and Electrical Engineering, Kyushu University. I received ME and PhD from Nara Institute of Science and Technology (NAIST) in 2016 and 2019, respectively. My research interests lie in computer vision, especially in light transport analysis and computational photography.
